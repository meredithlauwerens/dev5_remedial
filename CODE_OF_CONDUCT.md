# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for all participants. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for all members of our community and the consequences for unacceptable behavior.

## Our Values

We believe in:
- **Respect**: Treating all individuals with dignity and consideration
- **Inclusivity**: Welcoming people of all backgrounds, identities, and experiences
- **Collaboration**: Working together constructively to achieve shared goals
- **Integrity**: Acting honestly and taking responsibility for our actions
- **Professionalism**: Maintaining high standards in our interactions and work

## Expected Behavior

All community members are expected to:

- **Be Respectful**: Treat others with courtesy and kindness. Listen actively and consider different perspectives.
- **Be Inclusive**: Welcome and support people of all backgrounds. Use inclusive language and respect people's identities and experiences.
- **Communicate Clearly**: Communicate in a professional and constructive manner. Provide feedback that is helpful and delivered with good intent.
- **Take Responsibility**: Own your mistakes, apologize when necessary, and work to make improvements.
- **Collaborate Effectively**: Work together toward shared goals. Support team members and contribute to a positive environment.
- **Respect Boundaries**: Respect the personal space, time, and privacy of others.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are unacceptable and will not be tolerated:

- **Harassment**: Unwelcome conduct, comments, or advances based on protected characteristics (race, gender, age, religion, sexual orientation, disability, etc.)
- **Discrimination**: Treating individuals unfairly based on protected characteristics
- **Bullying**: Deliberately intimidating, threatening, or marginalizing others
- **Disrespect**: Dismissive, condescending, or hostile communication
- **Exclusion**: Intentionally excluding people from conversations or activities based on any characteristic
- **Inappropriate Content**: Sharing offensive, explicit, or inappropriate materials
- **Retaliation**: Taking adverse action against someone for reporting concerns or participating in an investigation
- **Violation of Privacy**: Sharing personal information without consent or violating confidentiality

## Reporting Concerns

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it:

1. **Direct Communication** (if safe): Address the concern directly with the individual involved
2. **Report to Leadership**: Contact a project lead, manager, or designated point of contact
3. **Formal Report**: Submit a written report documenting the incident, including:
   - What happened
   - When and where it occurred
   - Who was involved
   - Any witnesses
   - Actions you've taken so far

## Confidentiality & Non-Retaliation

- All reports will be treated with confidentiality and discretion
- We prohibit retaliation against anyone for reporting a concern in good faith
- We are committed to investigating all reports thoroughly and fairly
- We will take appropriate action based on the findings

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:
- A private conversation to clarify expectations
- A formal warning
- Temporary removal from community activities
- Permanent removal from the community
- Other consequences as appropriate

The severity of the response will be proportionate to the violation and may consider:
- The nature and severity of the behavior
- Whether it was a first offense or repeated behavior
- The impact on the affected individual(s)
- The individual's willingness to correct their behavior

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All community interactions (meetings, chats, emails, comments, etc.)
- Project spaces (repositories, forums, documentation, etc.)
- Events and gatherings
- Both online and in-person interactions

## Acknowledgment

By participating in this community, you agree to uphold and follow this Code of Conduct. We appreciate your commitment to maintaining a positive, respectful, and inclusive environment.
